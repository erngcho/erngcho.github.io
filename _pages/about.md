I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am fortunate to be advised by [Tim Dettmers](https://timdettmers.com/about/).

Before CMU, I was a researcher at Samsung Research, where I worked with [Yongkweon Jeon](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=dKQwu0YAAAAJ&hl=ko). I completed my M.S. in Computer Science at Korea University under the guidance of [Hyunwoo J. Kim](https://www.hyunwoojkim.com/people#h.6qdzg15coqu0).
